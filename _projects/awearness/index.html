effortlessly track mood

Mood tracking doesn't mean that you have to log how you feel all day. AWEARNESS uses a wearable mood tracker that detects physiological measures of mood.

find patterns

Look for correlations between your mood and the time, activities, and weather. AWEARNESS pulls events from your calendar so that you don't have to input activities manually.

share data

Seeking help can be hard. That's why you can set up AWEARNESS to automatically notify your mental health professional if your mood dips for two weeks.
