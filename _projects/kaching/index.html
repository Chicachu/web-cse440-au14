OVERVIEW

Spending on small discretionary items adds up resulting in not being able to spend on larger items that you actually want.

Ka-ching allows you to solely track discretionary spending, highlight a concrete goal to save towards, and help visualize the impact of your smaller purchases.

Take control of your spending and control where your money goes.

Designate Discrentionary

Easily designate which spending is discretionary.

Add Goal

Set tangible goals and visualizes progress towards your goal.

View Progress

Visually review your year, month, or week spending.
